About CiviMech Engineering

We feel Pleasure to Introduce Ourselves as engineering Construction Company in the form of “CIVIMECH ENGINEERING CO. We have been serving in the field of execution of Ducting, Piping (fabrication & Erection), Heavy Structural (fabrication & Erection), Scaffolding and Painting work as per clients requirements / specifications.

The strength and success of the company can be measured by the many top quality job they’ve completed, on time, with consistent client satisfaction. CIVIMECH ENGINEERING CO. takes pride in its teamwork, collaboration methodology, and capacity initiatives. Specializing in quality, cost-effective projects across a number of construction fields – structure fabrication & erection, piping fabrication & erection, scaffolding and painting job.

We endeavor to provide efficient leadership encourage creativity and training to keep pace with latest advance technology for bilateral growth. Our company believes in maintaining highest safety standards at site and also in zero accident and maximum productivity.
